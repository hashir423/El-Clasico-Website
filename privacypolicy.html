El Clasico - Privacy Policy

Summary

"El Clasico" is committed to protecting your personal data and handling it responsibly. This policy covers the personal data that El Clasico collects whenever you interact with us, including when you use our websites and apps, when you attend events, and when you correspond with us (such as by email or over the phone). It also covers personal data that we may receive from third parties.

Personal data El Clasico collects from you

We collect personal data from your interactions with us, such as when you engage with our websites and apps, attend an event at our Stadium, or when you email or phone us. The personal data we collect from you includes: the name and contact details that you provide when you register as a member with us your payment and address details your marketing preferences, including any consents you have given us your browser or device information information about your use of our websites and apps your communications with us information about your attendance at El Clasico events, such as matches, concerts and conferences held at our Stadium. Photos and video footage of you may be taken at these events

Personal data El Clasico receives from third parties

Sometimes we receive personal data from third parties, in particular: our commercial partners provide us with your name and contact details if you’ve asked to receive information from El Clasico.
